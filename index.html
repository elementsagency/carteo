How it works

Three steps. We do most of them.

The whole process is designed so you do as little as possible.

1

You send us your menu

A photo, a PDF, a Word doc, a WhatsApp message. Whatever you have. Add photos and let us know your colours and style preferences.

That's your job done.

2

We design, translate & build

We turn your menu into a beautiful, mobile-first digital menu. We apply your branding, translate it into the languages you need, and set up hosting.

No back-and-forth. No dashboards. We handle it all.

3

You get your QR code

We send you a QR code — ready to print and place on tables. Guests scan it and your menu is right there on their phone.

Most menus live within 3–5 days of receiving your content.
